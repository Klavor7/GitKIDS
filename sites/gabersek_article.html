Synopsis

In the mystical realm of Shrek's swamp, our favorite ogre finds himself facing an unexpected challenge when the Teletubbies, led by a mischievous figure named Tinky Winky, accidentally stumble into his territory. What starts as a whimsical misunderstanding quickly escalates into a clash of worlds, as the Teletubbies, with their colorful and playful nature, unwittingly disrupt the peaceful life Shrek has built for himself.

Cast
